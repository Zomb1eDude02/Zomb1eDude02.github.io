About Me...

Hi, I'm Gerry, I'm an amateur photographer just trying to have fun. I originally started getting into photography with my old phone, a Google Pixel 3a, but now I mainly use a Canon EOS 4000D. I also love to try taking experimental photos with things such as the Gameboy Camera. I am usually always open to try new things. I also like to make small video edits of my life every once in a while.
